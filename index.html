Hello, I'm Miles - your travel concierge × AVIS BUDGET GROUP

Twelve mockups across six transformation concepts — discovery, bundling, destination, loyalty, partnerships, the counter. Please contribute your feedback to this idea by emailing brianbishop@adobe.com

1 — Discovery becomes conversation

The static where/when/class form gives way to natural language. Miles returns a contextualized recommendation, then keeps the conversation going.

1A
Conversational hero

The static where/when/class form gives way to natural language. Miles returns a contextualized recommendation with reasoning visible.
1B
Conversation refines

Miles keeps the conversation going. Refine, compare alternatives, ask the why — without losing the trip context.

2 — Bundles understand your trip

Add-ons stop being checkout-time afterthoughts. Trip kits replace upsells; insurance gets explained instead of ambushed.

2A
Trip kit bundle

Add-ons stop being checkout-time afterthoughts. Once Miles knows the trip, snow chains + ski rack + GPS become one coherent kit.
2B
Insurance, only what you need

The honest insurance moment. Explained, contextualized to what your card already covers, sold without the upsell ambush.

3 — The trip extends past the booking

After Reserve, Miles doesn't disappear. Route, weather, things to do, and live in-trip nudges turn the rental into a journey.

3A
Destination companion

Once 'Reserve' is clicked, Miles doesn't disappear until pickup. Route, weather, and things to do at the destination — the trip extends.
3B
In-trip live companion

Mobile context, mid-drive. Miles surfaces timely intel — and turns add-on opportunities into cost savings instead of upsell ambushes.

4 — Loyalty becomes a journey

Avis Preferred reimagined as a quest — progress to status, miles framed as journey, achievement badges. Gamification with strategic substance.

4A
Loyalty becomes a journey

Avis Preferred reimagined as a quest — progress to status, miles framed as journey, achievement badges. Gamification with substance.
4B
Quest detail

Click into a single quest. Visualized progress, route on the map, what's left to earn — gamification with strategic substance.

5 — Partnerships compound the value

Aramark destinations bundled into one transaction. Cross-brand routing inside ABG when Avis isn't the right fit.

5A
Destination bundle (Aramark)

Vehicle stops being the product. With Aramark partnership, Miles bundles car + lodging + dining as one transaction at national parks.
5B
Cross-brand intelligence

Miles recognizes when Avis isn't the right fit and routes within the ABG portfolio. Loyalty earned across Avis, Budget, and Zipcar.

6 — The counter disappears

Counter-free pickup. Pre-inspected drop-off. The two highest-friction moments, dissolved.

6A
Counter-free pickup

The highest-friction moment, dissolved. Walk to the stall, check the car in-app, drive off — no counter line, no upsell ambush.
6B
Effortless drop-off

Pre-return inspection in app. Fuel-up tip that saves real money. Instant receipt. The drop-off anxiety, gone.
